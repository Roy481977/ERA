## Intent

IP-001 captured *what* ERA believes. This package begins the layer beneath it:
*why* attachment forms, expressed as candidate axioms and the discoveries that
reduced the philosophy toward them. It is research, not canon, and it is
explicitly allowed to pressure the Constitution.

**Discovery vs. explanation.** A *discovery* changes the philosophy (adds,
removes, replaces, or compresses a truth). An *explanation* only states an
existing truth more clearly. Only discoveries appear in §2 (Compression Events).
Explanations live inside the axioms and reasoning they support. This keeps the
repository growing through genuine progress, not elaboration.

## 1. Candidate Axioms

Confidence scale: **Strong** (survived repeated challenge) · **Moderate**
(converged but contested in part) · **Exploratory** (proposed, not yet tested).

### A1 — The externalized, vulnerable self
The self extends into what it marks; once part of the self lives outside the
self, it can be lost — and that exposure is a *source* of attachment, not a side
effect.
- **Confidence:** Strong.
- **Supporting reasoning:** Self-expansion and the "extended self"; identity is
  incorporated into places, groups and possessions. Vulnerability is what turns
  mild preference into deep feeling — you can lose part of yourself.
- **Open objections:** Whether *vulnerability* is truly inside this axiom or is a
  distinct axiom of its own (see RQ-2).
- **Constitutional implications:** Grounds ownership, identity, legacy; and
  strengthens honest-history/scars (IP-001 AT-7) by giving loss a mechanism.

### A2 — Recognition
The need to be acknowledged by a regard one cannot generate for oneself.
Working definition: a regard that **(1) channels a genuine valuing perspective**
and **(2) is independent of the present self.** Splits into **Internal**
Recognition (the living world of the Club registers the player) and **External**
Recognition (real people do).
- **Confidence:** Strong (the need); Moderate (the two-clause definition —
  clause 1 is contested, see RQ-3).
- **Supporting reasoning:** Recognition/"mattering" is a fundamental human need;
  it has an irreducible second-person structure — you cannot recognize yourself
  into mattering. Its four necessary conditions — **contingent, specific,
  unsolicited, revocable** — all reduce to "independent of the present self"
  (see CE-4). **Approval is not recognition:** a world that can only approve is
  rewarding, not recognizing; recognition must be able to turn negative
  (criticism, resentment, notoriety).
- **Open objections:** Is clause (1) required, or is independence-from-the-
  present-self sufficient on its own? Grandfather-letter and old-notebook cases
  show recognition needs no *present* mind — but both still have a *real* mind at
  the origin. Unresolved (RQ-3).
- **Constitutional implications:** Creates a new bedrock principle absent from
  IP-001; demotes Memory to a *mechanism* that expresses Recognition; promotes
  the Human Test to a load-bearer (it is the tether that keeps Internal
  Recognition channelling real human regard); tentatively resolves IP-001 OQ-2
  (belonging does **not** require multiplayer).

### A3 — Meaning
Significance is made by forming experience into narrative; it can be wholly
solitary, witnessed if only by the future self.
- **Confidence:** Strong.
- **Supporting reasoning:** People find profound meaning alone (the diary, the
  cabin, the planted tree). Narrative always has an implied audience — often
  one's later self — so meaning is *self-social across time*, not asocial.
- **Open objections:** None currently material.
- **Constitutional implications:** Separates cleanly from Recognition (CE-3) and
  re-sorts the two memories: **player→Club memory = Meaning** (narrative,
  solitary); **Club→player memory = Recognition** (needs the other mind).

### A4 — Independence (the player is never the sole author)
The Club has agency and momentum the player does not control. Reality, time,
culture, other people, emergence and history continuously co-author it, and it
moves on its own — it never waits for the player.
- **Confidence:** Strong.
- **Supporting reasoning:** Generalises "reality is co-author" (IP-001 AT-6):
  reality is one instance of a broader truth. Independence is the *ground of
  Recognition* — only an independent world can supply a regard independent of the
  present self. It also generates wonder, humility, and *un-chosen loss* (the old
  hero fading from the terraces' talk whether the player wills it or not).
- **Open objections:** Its tension with football's competitiveness/"grown vs
  built" (IP-001 OQ-3) is unresolved.
- **Constitutional implications:** Replaces/subsumes AT-6 ("reality is
  co-author"); creates a principle beneath "The Club is Alive" (AT-14/AT-15);
  connects to the arrow-of-time question (OQ-4).

### Candidate root (Exploratory) — the self extending past its boundary
A proposed single generative act beneath A1–A4: attachment is the self reaching
past its own edge in four directions — into matter (A1), into other regard (A2),
into its own time (A3), into a world it does not control (A4) — with
**vulnerability as the toll charged in every direction.**
- **Confidence:** Exploratory. **Not yet a discovery** (see RQ-1).
- **Test it must pass:** it must *exclude* something (it does: it forbids
  frictionless power-fantasy, pure optimization, pure collection — anything that
  keeps the self bounded and safe) **and** predict a *specific* ERA truth we have
  not already derived. Until it predicts something new, it is elegance, not a
  find.

### Meta-truth — attachment is a sustained tension
Attachment is A1–A4 held in permanent, unresolved tension (self yet not
controlled; permanent yet changing; mine yet shared; safe yet at stake). The
tensions are emotional *generators*, not implementation problems, and should
remain permanently unresolved.
- **Confidence:** Strong.

## 2. Compression Events

Discoveries that made the philosophy *smaller*. (Explanations excluded by rule.)

- **CE-1 — Recognition moved beneath Memory.** Memory is *evidence*; Recognition
  is the *need*. "The Club remembers me" matters because it proves "I mattered."
  Memory demoted from principle to mechanism.
- **CE-2 — Investment demoted.** "Investment creates ownership" replaced by the
  deeper "we bond to what holds our irreversible mark and reflects it back" —
  which explains *why* some investments become part of us and others are
  forgotten (retention + reflection).
- **CE-3 — Meaning separated from Recognition.** Two mechanisms, not one:
  Meaning is narrative and solitary-capable; Recognition is social and needs
  another mind. Cleanly re-sorted the two memories.
- **CE-4 — The four conditions collapsed into one.** Contingent, specific,
  unsolicited and revocable are not four requirements but four faces of a single
  one: *a regard independent of the present self.*
- **CE-5 — The Independence collapse.** Four things held separately — reality as
  co-author, emergence, the world's momentum, and "aliveness from the
  uncontrolled" — collapse into one axiom, **Independence** ("the player is never
  the sole author"), which then turns out to be the *ground* beneath Recognition.

*(Candidate, not yet a compression event: the "self past its boundary" root —
held in §1 and RQ-1 until it earns discovery status.)*

## 4. Active Research Questions

Only genuinely unresolved questions.

- **RQ-1 — Is there a deeper root beneath A1–A4?** Candidate: "the self extends
  past its boundary." Resolves only if it predicts one specific ERA truth not
  already derived; otherwise drop it. *(This is the compression event Roy still
  believes exists.)*
- **RQ-2 — Does vulnerability break out as its own axiom,** or stay inside A1?
- **RQ-3 — Is Internal Recognition sufficient on independence alone,** or does it
  require clause (1) — channelling a genuine valuing perspective? (Roy leans
  independence-sufficient; CKO argues a real perspective must be in the causal
  history, carried by the Human Test.)

*Still parked from IP-001 (not duplicated here):* OQ-3 (grown vs
competitiveness), OQ-4 (arrow of time), OQ-5 (newcomers vs veterans), OQ-6

## 5. Rejected or Demoted Ideas

Preserved with reasons. All were PROPOSED material; demotions are reversible.

- **Memory as a first principle** → **DEMOTED** to a mechanism expressing
  Recognition and Meaning (CE-1).
- **"Investment creates ownership" as fundamental** → **DEMOTED** to a mechanism;
  replaced by "irreversible mark, reflected back" (CE-2).
- **"Reality is the Club's co-author" as the principle** → **DEMOTED** to one
  *instance* of "the player is never the sole author" (CE-5). Candidate supersede
  of AT-6 on ratification.
- **"Recognition requires another *present* conscious mind"** → **REJECTED**;
  replaced by "a regard independent of the present self" (channelling a genuine
  valuing perspective).
- **"Belonging requires real human presence / multiplayer"** → **DEMOTED** from a
  blocking assumption to "External amplifies Internal"; belonging is a property of
  a living Club (tentative, via OQ-2 resolution).

## 6. Candidate Ratifications

Discoveries mature enough to *eventually* become constitutional — **all awaiting
Roy's explicit ratification. None is canonical.**

- **CR-1 — Recognition is a fundamental need, distinct from Memory.** (Strong)
- **CR-2 — Meaning (narrative) and Recognition (social) are distinct.** (Strong)
- **CR-3 — The player is never the sole author; the Club is Independent.**
  Supersedes "reality is co-author." (Strong)
- **CR-4 — Recognition must be contingent, specific, unsolicited and revocable;
  approval is not recognition.** (Strong)
- **CR-5 — Recognition is Internal (a property of the living Club) and External
  (real others); belonging does not depend on multiplayer.** (Strong–Moderate;
  would resolve OQ-2)
- **CR-6 — The Human Test is a constitutional load-bearer** — the tether that
  keeps Internal Recognition channelling real human regard. (Moderate–Strong)
- **CR-7 — Vulnerability is constitutive of attachment** (candidate standalone
  principle). (Moderate)
