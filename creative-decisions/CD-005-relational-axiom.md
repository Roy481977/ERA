## The decision

The primary relational axiom is **"deep attachment requires consequential agency
inside an independent world"** — *"the player shapes the Club but never solely
authors it"; stewardship, not sovereignty.* Chosen over the CKO's over-strong
formulation, *"you can only bond with what you do not fully control."*
*(Final wording open.)*

## Alternatives considered

- **"Non-control creates attachment"** — the CKO's linear-inversion formulation.
- **"Reality is the Club's co-author"** — true but too narrow (one instance).
- **Independence and Vulnerability as separate primitives.**

## Why this won

"Non-control" is **too strong** and was falsified by counterexample: humans
deeply love things they substantially control or create — a house, a painting, a
manuscript. The reason those are loved is that they *resisted* — the material,
the medium, the site pushed back. So the truth is not *less control* but
**agency meeting genuine otherness.** The relationship is an **inverted-U**: total
control yields a sterile object that only executes intention; zero control yields
spectatorship or powerlessness; attachment develops in the middle — *consequential
influence, never sovereignty* — with the peak sitting at a **different point per
domain** (direction wants high agency; culture wants high otherness). This single
law also **absorbs Independence and Vulnerability as derivations** and generalises
"reality is co-author," which is why it is a genuine compression and not a rename.

## What it rejects or supersedes

- **Rejects** "non-control / linear inversion."
- **Subsumes** AT-6 ("reality is co-author") as one instance.
- **Demotes** Independence and Vulnerability from primitives to derivations.

## Consequences

- Yields the design test: *Can the player shape this? Can it answer back?* — and
  its per-domain tuning.
- Establishes **stewardship, not sovereignty** as the player–Club relationship.
- Requires the living world (town, residents, interwoven lives) to stay
  **independent** — not a mood-ring of the player's results.
