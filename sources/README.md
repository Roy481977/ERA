# ERA — Source Materials (verbatim, pending distillation)

**Status: SOURCE MATERIAL — preserved verbatim, unclassified, NOT canonical.**
These are Roy's primary ERA documents, handed to the CKO on 2026-07-19 to
document the whole project. Originals are never edited. They will be distilled
into classified Import Packages (PROPOSED) and reconciled with IP-001 / IP-002;
nothing here becomes canon without Roy's explicit ratification.

Each file is stored as the original plus a plain-text extraction (`.txt`) for
search and diffing.

## The documents

1. **`design-bible-v0.1.pdf`** — *ERA Design Bible, Foundational Edition v0.1.*
   The most authoritative artifact. Nine volumes: the Thesis; the Emotional
   Promise (**five core emotions:** Ownership, Delight, Competition, Attachment,
   Expression); **The Constitution — seven design laws, each with a design
   test**; Experience Architecture (core loop, four rhythms, player-stories);
   the Living Club; Weather/Time/Real-World; AI as a World-Building Capability;
   From Vision to Mechanics (an eight-step design chain); and an Immediate Design
   Agenda with open working questions.

2. **`book-of-era-edition-I-v1.2.docx`** — *The Book of ERA, Edition I v1.2 —
   "The District Awakens."* The living-world / district layer: "constitutional
   discoveries" about place (the stage before the actors; the theatre without an
   audience; ~20–30% always beyond view; living density), Emotional Geography,
   Camera Philosophy (three zooms), district composition, landmarks, daily
   rhythms, match day, a list of canonical draft residents, a resident design
   template, visual direction, and open questions.

3. **`era-the-beginning-for-gamma.docx`** — *"ERA — The Beginning."* The
   manifesto / pitch: football is remembered in eras, not seasons; the greatest
   achievement is creating an era, not winning one; reality is shared, what you
   build from it is yours.

4. **`proposal-chatgpt-chat.pdf`** — *"My proposal."* The raw ChatGPT
   conversation (the one Roy was losing). Contains the football-district visual
   design (immediate/second/third rings, natural edges, four visual-identity
   rules, density, Nintendo/Family-Island references) **and** an earlier
   strategic layer (a "real-world-events platform" vision, an addictiveness
   framing, and a fun-first discovery method). Raw — mixes strong canon
   candidates with process and venting.

## Relationship to the existing repository (to be worked out in distillation)

The Design Bible carries **its own Constitution (seven design laws)** and **five
core emotions** — a *second constitutional thread* alongside IP-001 (philosophy)
and IP-002 (attachment axioms). They are deeply compatible but not identical and
**must be reconciled, never silently merged.**

Strong alignments already visible:
- "Reality Is Shared and Immutable" ≈ IP-002 *reality co-authors* / AR.
- "The world must continue without the player" ≈ the **Independence** axiom (AR).
- The eight-step design chain ≈ GOVERNANCE §4 design-governance trace.
- "The institution persists / not a save file" ≈ IP-001 Core Loop & "the Club is
  never finished."
- "Ownership emerges through decisions and taste" ≈ IP-001 AT-5 authorship /
  IP-002 AR ("shape but never solely author").

Contradictions to resolve (flagged, **not** merged):
- **Addiction vs. attachment.** The proposal chat asks "why is this game
  *addictive*"; IP-001 AT-1 holds the strongest emotion is *attachment, not
  addiction*. Direct tension.
- **Football as constitutive vs. "the first skin."** The proposal floats "the
  Clash of Clans of real-world events — football is just the first skin"; the
  Design Bible thesis and our axioms treat football as constitutive, not
  swappable. Strategic fork.
- **Vocabulary / structure.** Five emotions (incl. Delight, Expression) vs.
  IP-001's seven pillars; two different "Constitutions" (concrete design-law vs.
  philosophical). Neither is wrong; they need mapping.
