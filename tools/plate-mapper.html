How

Path: click points, dbl-click/Enter to finish, Esc cancel, Backspace undo point.
Place: pick from the dropdown, click the plate.
Obscure: click around an area the camera can't see (behind a house); dbl-click/Enter to close it. Anyone standing there is hidden.
Lamp: click to drop a lamp post (drag to place). A lamp inside an obscured zone hides its post but still casts light.
Select: drag points, pins or lamps; Backspace deletes.
Wheel = zoom, drag empty = pan.
