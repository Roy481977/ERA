How

Path (line): click points, dbl-click/Enter to finish, Esc cancel, Backspace undo point. A thin walk-line, taggable from→to.
Pathway (area): click around a walkable surface — the ring road, the plaza, a wide apron — dbl-click/Enter to close. Anyone may wander anywhere inside it (not just along a line).
Place: pick from the dropdown, click the plate.
Door: click once on each door. It's where a resident steps inside the nearest building (and out again) — so nobody stands awkwardly in a doorway. Mark them all.
House: click around a house's footprint, dbl-click/Enter to close. It becomes an occluder: anyone walking behind it is hidden by it (depth-sorted), so figures pass behind houses instead of over them.
Obscure: a general area the camera can't see past (a tunnel mouth, deep shade) — anyone inside is hidden. Use House for buildings; this is for the rest.
Water: outline the river's edge; it ripples in the game.
Stands: outline the terracing; the game-night crowd fills inside it.
Fence: click along a fence line. Low (dashed) = hop-over where a path crosses; wall (solid) = blocks and hides who's behind.
Light: click to drop a street light — a visible post with a warm head that glows at night (drag to place).
Lantern: click to drop a lantern — its light only shows at night and nothing of it appears by day; the post is never drawn in-game (the dashed ring here is just so you can see where it sits).
Select: drag points, pins, lights or doors; Backspace deletes.
Wheel = zoom, drag empty = pan.
